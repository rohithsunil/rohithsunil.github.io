Project Details

I was a website developer and graphic designer for 404 Divided. I designed a mobile-friendly e-commerce website that includes payment gateways and shipment tracking. I integrated the website to Facebook's business suite, which enabled the ability to shop directly from the Instagram page. The plugins set up let the business easily track sales and inventory from an intuitive control panel. The brand logo, hem tags, packaging, t-shirt designs, and promotional materials for social media were all created in collaboration with my colleague Noel Dominic.
